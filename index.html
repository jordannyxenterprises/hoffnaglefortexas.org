About

Meet Jordan Hoffnagle

I’m Jordan Hoffnagle, a father, community member, and conservative Republican running for Texas House District 100 because families deserve a representative who listens, shows up, and fights for common sense.

Like many Texans, I’ve watched everyday families deal with rising costs, schools drifting away from parents, and government growing more intrusive while delivering fewer results. I’m not running for politics as usual. I’m running to defend parental rights, practice fiscal responsibility, support public safety, and stand on constitutional principles.

I believe parents, not bureaucrats, should decide what’s best for their children. I believe hard-working Texans deserve to keep more of what they earn. And I believe representatives should answer to their constituents, not special interests.

This campaign is about restoring trust, protecting Texas values, and making sure District 100 has leadership that’s steady, accountable, and focused on results.

Priorities

Parental Rights

Parents should lead their child’s education and well-being, not distant administrators.

Fiscal Responsibility

Cut waste, prioritize essentials, and respect taxpayers.

Safe Communities

Support law enforcement and policies that keep neighborhoods secure.
